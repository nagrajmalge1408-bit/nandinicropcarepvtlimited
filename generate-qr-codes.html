📋 Instructions

How to Use

  1. Print QR Codes: Click "Print All QR Codes" to open the print preview with all product QR codes
  2. Download Individual: Click the download icon on any QR code card to save it individually
  3. Scan: Use any smartphone camera or QR scanner to scan the code
  4. View Product: Scanning will redirect to the complete product detail page (update yoursite.com in products.js)
  5. Print Settings:
    • Paper size: A4
    • Orientation: Portrait
    • Margins: 10mm
    • Colors: On (to ensure QR code readability)
